Wine Enthusiast's Highest Rated Wine in Virginia and 2013 Rising Star Winner.

Featuring almost 1,300 acres of breathtaking scenery, Trump Winery is situated in the heart of the Monticello Wine Trail in Charlottesville, Virginia. Just a few miles from Thomas Jefferson's Monticello, Trump Winery follows Jefferson's footsteps in making new world wines inspired by the French wine regions of Bordeaux and Champagne. With a deep sense of place we aim to tie together terroir and innovation. We know that any visit to Trump Winery will be a treasured experience.
